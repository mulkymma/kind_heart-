Our Story

Friends Of Kheyr Foundation, Non-Profit Organization

Friends of kheyr foundation is a youth founded initiative founded in 2021 with the aim of alleviating the needs of orphans and vulnurable individuals.

Our Mission

The mission of Friends of Kheir (FoK) is to alleviate the needs of orphans, widows, and the less fortunate in society, fostering positive communities. Dedicated to empowering marginalized groups and addressing basic needs, FoK aims to create lasting impact through collaborative efforts, support from well-wishers, and partnerships with similar initiatives. With a commitment to social justice and empathy, FoK strives to go beyond immediate assistance, delivering sustained support to those overlooked in society.
